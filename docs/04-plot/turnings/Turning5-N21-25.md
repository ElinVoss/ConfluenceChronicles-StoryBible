## SECTION 2: N22 — "LOYALTY TESTED" (CHAPTERS 1-15)

### **N22 OVERVIEW**

**Novella Theme:** Loyalty tested through sacrifice, **Rennik dies** (Hollowing), Jhace accepts terminal fate  
**Tone:** Grim sacrifice; loyalty demonstrated through action; calm before apocalyptic storm  
**Key Events:** **Rennik dies** from terminal Hollowing while healing Miri, Jhace fully accepts using Confluence will kill him, loyalty tested and proven through sacrifices, final preparations intensify  
**POV Distribution:** Jhace (9 chapters), Rennik (3 chapters including death), Brighlee (2 chapters), Selwin (1 chapter)

---

### **CHAPTER STRUCTURE (ABBREVIATED):**

**Chapter 1: JHACE — "INITIAL ASSAULT"**
- Four-faction assault begins; brutal opening fighting
- Resistance holds outer defenses barely; casualties: ~50 dead first day
- Rennik's medical center overwhelmed immediately

**Chapter 2: RENNIK — "MEDICAL CRISIS"**
- Performing triage constantly; Soulpulse overuse extreme
- Vein-burn spread past shoulders into chest; hands permanently cold
- Knows: Approaching terminal Hollowing stage

**Chapter 3: JHACE — "TACTICAL ADAPTATION"**
- Uses all four principles in combat rotation:
  - Form barriers vs. Xilcore's geometric attacks
  - Wholeness healing vs. Blemo's flames
  - Purity cleansing vs. Leesa's mental intrusions
  - Entropy disruption vs. Seeri's despair auras

**Chapter 4: RENNIK — "OVEREXTENSION"**
- Mass casualty event: Building collapse, 30 wounded
- Pushes beyond limits to save all 30; succeeds but collapses
- Symptoms: Flat affect, conviction failing, internal coldness spreading

**Chapter 5: JHACE — "RENNIK'S CONDITION"**
- Discovers Rennik near-Hollowed; orders complete rest
- Rennik resists: "People are dying."
- Jhace: "That's an order. You've given enough."

**Chapters 6-8: RENNIK'S DEATH SEQUENCE (CLIMAX)**

**Chapter 6: RENNIK POV — "ONE LAST PATIENT"**
- Despite orders, hears Miri injured in skirmish (needs immediate healing)
- Can't ignore: "Just one more. I can save one more."

**Chapter 7: RENNIK POV — "HOLLOWING COMPLETE"**
- Uses Wholeness to heal Miri's injuries completely
- Mid-healing, terminal Hollowing completes:
  - Veins blacken fully, body freezes internally
  - Conviction: Total failure—no longer believes healing matters
- Dies peacefully with hands on Miri; successful final heal
- **Ninth major death**

**Chapter 8: JHACE POV — "NINTH LOSS"**
- Finds Rennik dead, Miri healed and crying beside him
- Rennik's expression: Peaceful, finally at rest
- Jhace: Brief grief, processes quickly (emotionally numb now)
- Buries Rennik with full honors

**Chapter 9: JHACE — "ACCEPTING TERMINAL FATE"**
- Post-Rennik: No medic left, no safety net
- Realizes: If uses Confluence in final battle, will die
- Fully accepts terminal sacrifice: "My life for their freedom. Fair trade."

**Chapter 10: BRIGHLEE — "LOGISTICS COLLAPSE"**
- Medical capacity gone; wounded treated with basic first aid only
- Morale hit but survivors rally: "He died saving us. We honor that by continuing."

**Chapter 11: JHACE — "LOYALTY THROUGH SACRIFICE"**
- Observes survivors sacrificing for each other:
  - Fighters giving rations to civilians
  - Elderly volunteering for dangerous roles
- Loyalty proven through actions, not words

**Chapter 12: SELWIN — "DEFENSIVE ADJUSTMENTS"**
- Adapts defenses post-Rennik: No healing available
- More conservative tactics; fighters must avoid injury entirely

**Chapter 13: JHACE — "CALM BEFORE STORM"**
- Brief lull in fighting (factions regrouping for major push)
- Uses time to mentally prepare for Confluence use
- Visits all major survivors: Final words exchanged

**Chapter 14: BRIGHLEE — "INTELLIGENCE FINAL REPORT"**
- Confirms: All four gods personally arriving (not just lieutenants)
- Will join battle directly within days
- Jhace: "Good. Means I can face them all at once."

**Chapter 15: JHACE — "LOYALTY PROVEN" (CLIMAX)**
- War council: Everyone aware next phase = final showdown
- Jhace reveals plan: Will use Confluence to trap gods (likely fatal to him)
- Council unanimous: "We're with you. To the end."
- Everyone pledges loyalty through commitment to fight alongside him
- Final moment: "Then we fight. Together. One last time."

**Final Line (N22):**  
*"Loyalty was tested not by speeches but by sacrifice. Rennik gave his last breath. Jhace prepared to give his. And every survivor stood ready to give theirs. The final calm settled, and the storm awaited."*
