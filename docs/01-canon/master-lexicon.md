## TIDE (Day)

**Definition:** One rotation of Te'Oga, divided into five named periods based on forge/glass work cycles.

### Five Daily Periods:

1. **Kindling (Dawn)** — First Tide; call to work; heat-grid activates
2. **Pouring (Mid-morning)** — Second Tide; full work begins; peak energy
3. **Shaping (Noon)** — Third Tide; peak heat; maximum output
4. **Striking (Afternoon)** — Fourth Tide; refinement; precision work
5. **Cooling Hour / The Quench (Dusk)** — Fifth Tide; work winds down; heat-grid rests

### Usage in Prose:
- "By Kindling, the scaffold was down." (by dawn)
- "They worked through Shaping and into Striking." (through midday into afternoon)
- "The Quench brought reprieve." (evening came)

---

## ANNEAL (Week — 5 Days)

**Definition:** The five-day cycle required for proper annealing of glassworks. Each day has a craft-name:

| Order | Name | Meaning | Crafting Stage |
|-------|------|---------|----------------|
| 1 | **Heattide** | Preparation, gathering | Material assembly |
| 2 | **Pourtide** | Beginning work, molten state | Activation/ignition |
| 3 | **Shapetide** | Formation, the peak | Maximum effort |
| 4 | **Striketide** | Refinement, hammer and tool | Precision work |
| 5 | **Cooltide** | Settling, hardening, rest | Integration/completion |

### Usage in Prose:
- "On Striketide, Jhace realized the crack ran deeper." (fourth day of week)
- "They had until Cooltide to finish." (by end of week)
- "Meet me at Pourtide." (second day of week)

## CRUCIBLE (Month — 7 Anneals / 35 Tides)

# SECTION 2: SOULPULSE MAGIC SYSTEM

## Core Concept

**SoulPulse:** The capacity to persuade matter's **veins** (stress lines) through will, emotion, and conviction. Energy drawn from Te'Oga's vein-network (underground energy channels).

**Discovery:** Post-Piercing spontaneous awakening (Year 423, Casting 423)

---

## FOUR PRINCIPLES

### 1. FORM (Structure/Order)

**Function:** Shapes matter, creates geometric constructs, hardens defenses  
**Emotional Driver:** Desire for order, control, perfection  
**Physical Sign:** Veins glow blue-white, geometric patterns on skin  
**Visual:** Crystalline structures, perfect angles, rigid barriers  
**Cost:** Rigidity, brittleness if overused

**Examples:**
- Create crystalline barriers
- Shape tools/weapons from raw material
- Reinforce structures geometrically
- Lock objects in perfect alignment

**God:** Xilcore (embodies Form to absolute extreme)

---

### 2. WHOLENESS (Connection/Healing)

**Function:** Heals injuries, connects minds empathically, integrates systems  
**Emotional Driver:** Compassion, desire for unity, love  
**Physical Sign:** Veins glow golden, warmth radiating from hands  
**Visual:** Organic curves, flowing connections, golden light  
**Cost:** Emotional bleeding (feel others' pain), boundary dissolution

**Examples:**
- Heal wounds (physical/psychological)
- Sense others' emotions
- Create empathic networks
- Integrate broken systems

**God:** Leesa (embodies Wholeness to absolute extreme)

---

### 3. PURITY (Cleansing/Isolation)

**Function:** Burns impurities, severs connections, isolates perfection  
**Emotional Driver:** Desire for cleanliness, rejection of flaws  
**Physical Sign:** Veins glow white-hot, flames around extremities  
**Visual:** White flames, sterile cleansing, severed connections  
**Cost:** Cold isolation, inability to connect

**Examples:**
- Purge toxins/diseases
- Sever unwanted connections (mental/physical)
- Cleanse corruption
- Isolate perfection by burning away flaws

**God:** Blemo (embodies Purity to absolute extreme)

---

### 4. ENTROPY (Dissolution/Release)

**Function:** Decays matter, releases tension, accelerates endings  
**Emotional Driver:** Acceptance, surrender, peace with impermanence  
**Physical Sign:** Veins glow gray-mist, cold numbness  
**Visual:** Gray mist, peaceful dissolution, gentle decay  
**Cost:** Passivity, difficulty fighting/persisting

**Examples:**
- Accelerate decay (structures, bodies)
- Release tension/pain peacefully
- Induce acceptance/surrender
- Dissolve barriers through peaceful dissolution

**God:** Seeri (embodies Entropy to absolute extreme)

---

## CONFLUENCE (Fifth State)

**Definition:** All four principles used simultaneously—ultimate paradox state.

**Requirements:**
- Mastery of all four principles individually
- Paradox tolerance (holding opposites: order/chaos, connection/isolation)
- Extreme emotional/conviction intensity

**Effect:**
- Reality warping (principles merge, physics unstable)
- Traps entities in paradox (Fivefold Stasis)
- **Terminal Cost:** Calcification, inevitable death

**Users:**
- Jhace Torrins (spontaneous N05, mastered N25, sustained N26-N30)
- Four Gods cannot use (too absolute in single principles)

**Visual:** All four colors simultaneously (blue-white, golden, white-hot, gray-mist), reality shimmer, geometric-organic-flame-mist hybrid

---

## SOULPULSE TECHNIQUES

### Core Techniques:

**Velding:** Correct persuasion—guide, don't force (proper use)  
**Brasting:** Coercive forcing—leads to snaps and failures (improper use)

### Advanced Techniques:

**Binding-token:** Glass/metal coin etched to hold a micro-pattern  
**Resonance-stone:** Tuned mineral that carries a hum for a brief span  
**Blood-script:** Finger-drawn trace that "prints" a pattern into veins  
**Cadence:** Breath count during working (common: four-true, six-lock, eight-marry)  
**Dual-principle:** Combined persuasion (2 principles); powerful, expensive, risky  
**Threshold:** Personal inflection where capacity changes behavior  
**Rebound:** Backlash when a line slips; causes nosebleed or joint ache

---

## SOULPULSE COSTS & LIMITS

### Physical Costs:

**Vein-burn:** Visible darkening of veins from overuse
- Form: Geometric patterns
- Wholeness: Organic webs
- Purity: Straight lines
- Entropy: Dark blooms
- Progression: Hands → forearms → torso → neck → face → complete

**Hollowing:** Terminal exhaustion when conviction fails
- Stage 1: Vein-burn visible, minor tremors, fatigue
- Stage 2: Flat affect, conviction weakening, coldness spreading
- Stage 3: Total emotional numbness, physical collapse imminent
- Stage 4: Death from conviction failure (body gives up)

**Calcification:** Meta-Confluence terminal state (body petrifies)
- Only occurs with sustained Confluence use
- Irreversible, fatal
- Body hardens to stone-like state

### Psychological Costs:

**Conviction Drain:** Losing sense of purpose/meaning  
**Emotional Bleeding:** Feeling others' pain (Wholeness specific)  
**Isolation:** Inability to connect (Purity specific)  
**Passivity:** Difficulty persisting (Entropy specific)  
**Tremor:** Immediate physical cost—fingertip → forearm; worsens with complexity  
**After-heat:** Residual body warmth and fatigue post-working

### Limits:

- Requires emotional/conviction fuel (cannot use mechanically)
- Overuse leads to Hollowing (death from conviction failure)
- Confluence = terminal (body cannot sustain paradox long-term)
- Gods cannot die from Hollowing (infinite conviction), but can be killed by external force

# SECTION 12: WRITING CONSISTENCY RULES

## Temporal System

- NEVER use: week, month, year, hour, minute, Monday-Sunday, January-December
- ALWAYS use: anneal, crucible, casting, tide, breaths + named days/months from lexicon
- Date format: Tide/Anneal/Crucible/Casting (e.g., 3/5/7/428)

## Soulpulse System

- Always requires emotional/conviction fuel
- Costs escalate with use: vein-burn → Hollowing → calcification
- Confluence = terminal (cannot be used casually)
- Gods cannot use Confluence (too absolute in single principles)
- Show costs after every working (even minor tremor counts)

## Geography

- Northern = cold/mountains (Xilcore)
- Southern = warm/plains (Leesa)
- Eastern = hot/deserts (Blemo)
- Western = cool/mist (Seeri)
- Central = temperate/urban (contested)

## Language Style

- Keep forge/glass renaissance voice
- Prefer craft verbs: brace, true, square, purge, seat, temper, anneal
- Use color and count over numbers: "Heat to lemon; hold eight breaths"
- Show Soulpulse costs: tremor, vein-burn, hollowing, after-heat
- Avoid modernisms—use Substitution Table (Section 10)

## How to extend the **Regex** column (for linter precision)

Use the **Regex** column to define an explicit pattern for the linter. If empty, the linter auto-builds a pattern from the **Modern** term (with simple plural handling).

**Rules of the road**
- Patterns are compiled **case-insensitive**.
- Prefer explicit **word boundaries** (`\b`) to avoid false positives.
- Use `|` for alternatives, and `(?: … )` for non‑capturing groups if you need grouping.
- Whitespace is *not* special; use `\s+` where flexible spacing is allowed.
- Keep patterns **surgical**—avoid over-broad matches that could hit names or lore terms.

**Examples**
| Modern            | In-World                          | Regex                                        | Notes                    |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| week              | anneal/anneals                    | `\bweeks?\b`                               | simple plural            |
| hour              | tides/breaths (context)           | `\bhours?\b`                               | simple plural            |
| power grid        | heat-grid/pressure-lines          | `\b(power|electrical)\s+grid\b`           | synonyms + spacing       |
| phone / call / text | runner / bellman / notice slate | `\b(phone|call|text)(s|ed|ing)?\b`        | verb inflections         |
| police / cops     | guard cohort / Bureau agents      | `\b(police|cops)\b`                        | alternatives             |
