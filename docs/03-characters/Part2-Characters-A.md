## PROTAGONIST: JHACE TORRINS — Complete Arc N01-N30

### **Overview Snapshot**

**Age Progression:** 28 (N01) → 37 (N30)  
**Role Evolution:** Repair smith → Healer → Resistance leader → God-killer → Dead martyr  
**Power Evolution:** Instinctive Wholeness → Confluence (4 principles) → Meta-Confluence (5 principles, terminal)  
**Moral Evolution:** Innocent idealist → Pragmatic warrior → Morally compromised leader → Resolved martyr  
**Fatal Flaw:** Hyperresponsibility (believes every failure is his fault, cannot delegate, burns out)

---

### **TURNING ONE: GLASS (N01-N05) — Innocence to Devastation**

**N01: Grip That Holds**
- **State:** Isolated repair smith, competent but lonely
- **Discovery:** Instinctive healing ability awakens (Wholeness principle)
- **Relationship:** Meets Tiffani, partnership begins, mutual respect
- **Trauma:** Piercing catastrophe, witnesses mass death, infrastructure chaos
- **Psychological:** Survivor's guilt begins forming

**N02: Light Cracks Through**
- **State:** Processing trauma, learning Soulpulse basics from Tiffani
- **Discovery:** Four principles identified (Form, Wholeness, Purity, Entropy)
- **Relationship:** Love developing with Tiffani, intellectual/romantic bond
- **Victory:** Prevents major disaster (stress-riser), city saved, validation
- **Psychological:** Cautious hope, believes rebuilding possible

**N03: Alloy Folded Wrong**
- **State:** Confident, active in reconstruction efforts
- **Failure:** **Bram dies**—first major person Jhace fails to save despite healing attempt
- **Trauma:** Realizes Soulpulse has limits, not omnipotent
- **Relationship:** Tiffani comforts, teaches restraint
- **Psychological:** Hyperresponsibility flaw emerges (blames self for Bram)

**N04: Shatter From Heat**
- **State:** Overworked, near-Hollowing from excessive Soulpulse use
- **Failure:** Showcase collapse, Corlexi Hollows (Jhace witnesses horror of overuse)
- **Relationship:** Tiffani warns him about costs, urges rest
- **Psychological:** Stubbornness—refuses to stop despite warnings
- **Physical:** Vein-burn spreading, hands trembling

**N05: Steal Breaks Open (CLIMAX)**
- **State:** Exhausted, desperate, suppression device failing
- **Crisis:** Four gods awaken, world collapses into divine tyranny
- **Relationship:** **TIFFANI DIES**—killed by debris/divine awakening chaos, dies protecting Jhace
- **Trauma:** Soul-shattered, loses love of his life, foundational trauma
- **Discovery:** Confluence manifests spontaneously during grief (all four principles merge briefly)
- **Psychological:** Suicidal despair, barely functional, considers giving up
- **Resolution:** Vows to continue fight "for her," channels grief into purpose

**Turning One Endpoint:**
- **Physical:** Vein-burn moderate, exhausted
- **Emotional:** Devastated but determined
- **Moral:** Still idealistic, believes tyranny can be fought
- **Power:** Instinctive multi-principle use, Confluence proven possible
- **Relationships:** Tiffani **DEAD**, Chloen alive (lieutenant), Kylar alive (strategist)

### **TURNING TWO: STEEL (N06-N10) — Grief to Leadership**

**N06: Ash After Fire**
- **State:** Numb, hyperactive to avoid processing Tiffani's death
- **Activity:** Organizes survivors, establishes resistance nucleus (~100 people)
- **Relationship:** Visits Tiffani's makeshift memorial, whispers promises to continue
- **Psychological:** Grief-driven purpose, refuses to mourn properly
- **Power:** Uses multi-principle tactics Tiffani taught, honors her memory through action

**N07: Four Thrones Rise**
- **State:** Accepting leadership reluctantly, 200+ fighters now
- **Activity:** Studies god-domains, identifies ideological weaknesses
- **Relationship:** Remembers Tiffani's teachings about ethical restraint
- **Psychological:** Guilt as anchor (fights to honor her sacrifice)
- **Power:** Deliberate multi-principle rotation in combat

**N08: Caught Between Forms**
- **State:** Exploiting inter-god warfare, resistance growing
- **Victory:** Uses Confluence in combat successfully (non-fatal cost)
- **Relationship:** Marisse emerges as ethical advisor (reminds him of Tiffani)
- **Psychological:** Functional but fragile, grief unprocessed
- **Power:** Confluence controllable for short bursts

**N09: Forged in Defiance**
- **State:** 500+ fighters, public declaration of resistance
- **Relationship:** **Chloen dies**—third major loss (pattern emerging)
- **Trauma:** Realizes everyone close to him dies
- **Psychological:** Isolates emotionally (won't get close to prevent more pain)
- **Memory:** Remembers Tiffani saying "connection is strength, not weakness"

**N10: First Strike (CLIMAX)**
- **State:** Confident leader, 2,000 fighters
- **Victory:** Major operation succeeds, proves gods vulnerable
- **Relationship:** Whispers "for you, Tiffani" before operation
- **Psychological:** Grief channeled into tactical brilliance
- **Power:** Multi-principle mastery, Confluence used strategically

**Turning Two Endpoint:**
- **Physical:** Vein-burn worsening but manageable
- **Emotional:** Grief-driven, hyperactive, avoiding pain
- **Moral:** Ethical warfare, still restraint
- **Power:** Multi-principle expert, Confluence tactical weapon
- **Relationships:** Tiffani **DEAD (memory guides him)**, Chloen **DEAD**, Kylar alive, Marisse alive (ethical anchor)
