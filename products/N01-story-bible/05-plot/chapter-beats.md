# Chapter Beats — N01: "The Grip That Holds"

## Purpose

Chapter-by-chapter roadmap for drafting. Each entry includes POV, scene summary, emotional beat, cost/consequence, and hook to next chapter.

**Target:** 15 chapters × 3,000 words = 45,000 words total

---

## CHAPTER 1: The Warm Man (Jhace POV)

**Word Count:** ~3,000

### Scene Summary

Jhace begins his typical workday in Coreven—Underface, repairing marry-points on scaffold networks. A young worker (not Nael yet; different injury) falls and breaks their arm. Jhace heals instinctively, warmth spreading from chest to hands. The worker recovers quickly. Jhace feels exhausted but satisfied. Introduces Keev and Ranna (married scaffold workers) who witness the healing and react with awe and concern. Evening: Jhace remembers Bram's death; guilt and fear surface. Establishes his trauma anchor.

### Key Story Functions

- Introduce Jhace: compassionate, practical, physically oriented
- Demonstrate warmth-type healing: instinctive, effective
- Show community context: post-Piercing adjustment, magic emergence
- Establish Bram's death as trauma anchor (memory/flashback)
- Set up Jhace's fear of failure and loss

### Emotional Beat

Compassion drives Jhace to act, but fear lurks beneath. Healing feels right and terrifying simultaneously.

### Cost/Consequence

**Micro working:** Minor exhaustion, warmth depletion. No visible damage yet. Establishes baseline.

### Character Introductions

- Jhace Torrins (POV)
- Keev and Ranna Torsen (supporting)
- Bram Torrins (deceased; memory)

### Locations

- Coreven—Underface (scaffold networks)
- Jhace's workshop (brief)

### Hook to Next Chapter

Community gossip about magic users reaches Tiffani; she dismisses it as uncontrolled compared to her precision work.

## CHAPTER 2: Crystalline Clarity (Tiffani POV)

**Word Count:** ~3,000

### Scene Summary

Tiffani works in her glassworks, shaping a complex marry-point with unprecedented precision using magic. Cool, crystalline clarity fills her mind. Geometric patterns guide her hands. The piece is perfect. Lessin (apprentice) watches in awe. Later, Tiffani attends a Council administrative meeting where bureaucrats discuss magic regulation. She meets Adric Voss (bureaucrat, fearful of chaos) and Venn Eries (philosopher, calm voice). Mobel Kress is present but withdrawn, sitting apart. Tiffani notices but doesn't understand. Evening: Tiffani reflects on her mother's death in a structural collapse; her drive for perfection stems from fear of failure.

### Key Story Functions

- Introduce Tiffani: perfectionist, precision-oriented, controlled
- Demonstrate precision-type shaping: mentally focused, geometrically precise
- Show early Mobel isolation symptoms (subtle)
- Introduce Venn as philosophical voice
- Establish Council's inadequate response to magic
- Set up Tiffani's trauma anchor (mother's death)

### Emotional Beat

Pride in perfection masks fear of chaos. Control is Tiffani's shield against vulnerability.

### Cost/Consequence

**Micro working:** Mental exhaustion, brief coolness in fingertips. Subtle joint stiffness begins (dismissed).

### Character Introductions

- Tiffani Merrow (POV)
- Lessin Hode (apprentice)
- Adric Voss (bureaucrat)
- Venn Eries (philosopher)
- Mobel Kress (preservation worker, isolating)

### Locations

- Tiffani's Glassworks
- Council Administrative District (meeting room)

### Hook to Next Chapter

News spreads: scaffold collapse in Underface; child trapped. Tiffani hears rumors of "the warm man" saving lives.

## CHAPTER 3: The Burn Begins (Jhace POV)

**Word Count:** ~3,000

### Scene Summary

**INCITING INCIDENT.** Scaffold collapse in Underface during peak work hours (Shaping period). Child (Nael, 5-7 years old) trapped under twisted metal, bleeding, leg broken. Jhace arrives; crowd gathered, terrified. Heat-grid exposed (danger). Jhace stabilizes child with healing: warmth floods through him, pulling toward injuries. Bone knits, bleeding stops. Crowd watches in silence. Child clings to Jhace afterward, calls him "the warm man." Gratitude and awe from parents. BUT: Jhace feels wrong—pulling sensation won't stop, warmth turns to burning. Left forearm develops angry red disc (~1.5 cm): vein-burn appears for first time. Left hand begins subtle tremor. Community reaction: awe, gratitude, fear. Jhace realizes: magic costs.

### Key Story Functions

- **INCITING INCIDENT:** First major cost (vein-burn)
- Establish public magic use and social consequences
- Introduce Nael (child saved)
- Demonstrate grand working and its cost
- Shift tone: wonder → caution

### Emotional Beat

Heroism and terror collide. Jhace saves a life but realizes he's marked permanently.

### Cost/Consequence

**Grand working:** Vein-burn appears (~1.5 cm, left forearm). Left hand tremor begins. Exhaustion severe. Cost is visible and permanent.

### Character Introductions

- Nael (child saved)
- Enner Coil (senior inspector, witnesses event)

### Locations

- Underface (scaffold collapse site)
- Heat-grid access point (danger zone)

### Hook to Next Chapter

Council summons Jhace for interrogation. Tiffani hears about scaffold incident; curious about "the warm man."

## CHAPTER 4: Interrogation and Stiffness (Tiffani POV)

**Word Count:** ~3,000

### Scene Summary

Morning: Tiffani wakes with stiff fingers; dismisses it. Works through morning, hands slower than usual. Pain during fine work. Lessin notices, offers help; Tiffani refuses (pride). Afternoon: Council summons both Jhace and Tiffani (separately) for interviews about magic use. Adric Voss interrogates Jhace (off-page; Tiffani hears afterward). Tiffani's interview: Adric asks about precision shaping, wants documentation, regulations. Tiffani defends autonomy; sees regulations as fear-driven. Waiting room: Jhace and Tiffani meet for first time. Brief conversation; mutual respect. Jhace's forearm bandaged (vein-burn hidden). Tiffani notices his exhaustion. Evening: Tiffani's hands visibly stiff; fear creeps in. Tries to shape glass; pain increases. Stops, shaken.

### Key Story Functions

- Tiffani's cost begins (stiffness)
- Jhace and Tiffani meet; connection starts
- Council friction: bureaucracy vs. autonomy
- Parallel costs: vein-burn (Jhace) and stiffness (Tiffani)

### Emotional Beat

Control slipping. Tiffani's perfection challenged by her own body.

### Cost/Consequence

**Standard working (earlier):** Joint stiffness noticeable; pain during fine work. Fear begins.

### Character Interactions

- Tiffani and Jhace meet
- Tiffani and Adric Voss (conflict)
- Tiffani and Lessin (mentor/apprentice)

### Locations

- Tiffani's Glassworks
- Council Administrative District (Adric's office)

### Hook to Next Chapter

Community meeting called at Lattice Yards; magic use to be debated publicly. Both Jhace and Tiffani invited.

## CHAPTER 5: The Lattice Yards Debate (Jhace POV)

**Word Count:** ~3,000

### Scene Summary

Evening: Community gathers at Lattice Yards (open scaffold platform). Diverse crowd: healers, builders, skeptics, bureaucrats. Callai Vreen organizes meeting. Derris Haln argues for restrictions ("too dangerous"). Venn Eries mediates ("understand first"). Jhace attends; vein-burn bandaged but visible. Refuses healing requests from crowd (fear of worsening). Tiffani attends; observes from edge. Chloen (young worker Jhace healed once) demonstrates versatility: can heal AND shape. Crowd astonished. Derris: "This proves we don't understand it—too risky." Callai: "This proves we should embrace it—incredible potential." Venn: "This proves we must learn together." No resolution. Afterward: Jhace and Tiffani talk privately. He shows her vein-burn; she admits stiffness. Shared vulnerability. Connection deepens.

### Key Story Functions

- Community debate: philosophical positions emerge
- Chloen's versatility introduced (foreshadowing dual-principle)
- Jhace refuses healing (cost awareness growing)
- Jhace and Tiffani bond over shared costs
- Act I closes: costs undeniable, social tension rising

Isolation vs. connection. Jhace and Tiffani find understanding in each other.

### Cost/Consequence

**Tremor worsens** (Jhace); **stiffness persists** (Tiffani). Both aware costs are not temporary.

### Character Interactions

- Community ensemble (Callai, Derris, Venn, Chloen)
- Jhace and Tiffani (deeper connection)

### Locations

## CHAPTER 6: Shattered Perfection (Tiffani POV)

Lessin tries to console; Tiffani snaps at her (defensive). Attempts to recreate the piece; hands stiff, pain constant. Pushes through. Piece completed but flawed (visible imperfection). Tiffani nearly breaks emotionally; catches herself. Afternoon: Jhace visits glassworks (professional check on marry-points). Sees Tiffani's distress. Attempts to heal her hands; warmth feels wrong—joints resist. Magic doesn't work. Both frustrated. Jhace: "Some things can't be fixed." Tiffani: "Then what's the point?" Conversation turns vulnerable: what if costs are permanent? Evening: Tiffani stares at flawed piece. Accepts she'll never craft perfectly again. Grief.

**Word Count:** ~3,000

Bystanders injured (minor burns, cuts). Soress collapses; arms covered in burns (vein-burn equivalent but worse). Crowd terrified. Prett Mallin (traditional healer) treats bystanders; glares at Jhace ("This is what your kind does"). Jhace helps Soress; stabilizes him. Vein-burn throbs; tremor worsens.
